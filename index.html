Who We Are

A Movement Built on Compassion

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8, NITI Aayog–registered non-profit headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. Founded in 2020 by young changemakers, we work at the grassroots level across education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental conservation, and community development.

Our goal is simple: create opportunities for every underprivileged person to grow with dignity and respect. We are ISO 9001:2015 certified and driven by a family of over 250 permanent team members and thousands of passionate interns.

🏛️ NITI Aayog Registered ✅ ISO 9001:2015 Certified 📋 CSR-1 Approved 🤝 Section 8 Non-Profit

Our Initiatives

6 Projects, Endless Impact

Each project targets a unique social challenge — together forming a complete ecosystem of change.

🍚
Project Seva
Food & Essentials

Distributing meals, clothing, and daily essentials to underserved communities — ensuring dignity and warmth for those in need, regardless of their circumstances.

🥣 50,000+ meals distributed
📚
Project Bachpanshala
Child Education

Bridging the education gap for underprivileged children through digital literacy, life skills, and school support — nurturing dreams and building brighter futures in rural areas.

🎒 Children educated in rural areas
🐾
Project Jeev
Animal Welfare

Protecting, rescuing, and feeding stray animals every single day — promoting compassion toward all living beings and advocating for their safety and well-being.

🐕 50+ animals fed daily
💫
Project Udaan
Women Empowerment

Uplifting women through skill-building workshops, entrepreneurship programs, self-help group collaborations, and menstrual hygiene awareness — fostering financial independence and leadership.

👩 900+ girls empowered via digital campaigns
🌍
Project Prakriti
Environment

Protecting the environment through tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, and sustainable farming awareness — every sapling is a gift to future generations.

🌱 20,000+ saplings planted
🎓
Project Vikas
Youth & Skills

Empowering youth like you with real-world internships, professional skill-building, and meaningful social work experience — shaping India's next generation of changemakers.

🚀 30,000+ interns trained
